Welcome to SemToNotes

Semantic Topological Notes (SemToNotes) is a topological image annotation and image retrieval system written in JavaScript and XQuery/Java. It allows the analysis of spatio-topological relations between semantically enriched image areas.

SemToNotes was developed at the Institute of Humanities Computer Science at the University of Cologne as part of the DARIAH-DE Project (Phase II, March 2014 - October 2015).
